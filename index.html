ABOUT

WHY DESIGN?

I love design as it is the convergence of analytical problem-solving and artistic innovation. Design poses a unique challenge. The resulting visual experience not only mitigates an issue or addresses a need but also has the potential to evoke powerful emotions and make a lasting impact on people. In this way, design is an ever-evolving field with limitless capacity that stimulates my ambition and satisfies my desire for continuous growth.

I have not always been a UI/UX Designer. After graduating with an accounting degree and overcoming the grueling process of becoming a licensed Certified Public Accountant (CPA), I pursued a career at a fast-paced Fortune 100 companyâ€™s internal audit department. I am so grateful for the professional development, technical training, and soft skills gained through this experience, but perhaps the most valuable lesson was in self-discovery. Just because you excel at something does not mean you will feel happy and fulfilled doing it the rest of your life. I learned that my twenty-year-old self did not understand life as much as I thought I did when I ignored my lifelong interest in design and creative expression, instead opting for what I viewed as the rational, safe path. Because what could be more important than the guaranteed job security and financial stability of accountancy?

I do not hold this against myself. I was young and naive, but I nevertheless faced a multitude of overwhelming decisions with optimism, conviction, and determination to succeed. The challenges and triumphs of these transformative years allowed me to expand my perspective and critically evaluate my priorities and life goals. With this career change, I choose passion, authenticity, and expression.
